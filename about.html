About Us

Rafield Limited stands as an intermediary between its clients and the endless list of experienced web developers who are registered on our platforms.

We are a growing community of dedicated developers, commited to putting awesome touches to your web and mobile application development which will make your business or organizaton stand out. Our services also span Web Design/Development Training at the convinience of your home, as well as recruitinng skilled programmers for medium and large IT/Tech companies.

How We Operate

By standing as an intermediary, we seek the jobs from our clients and allocate them to our registered developers on our database based on the job description. Our system for allocating jobs is built on our unique "queue up" automatic system which assigns jobs automatically based on first-come first-serve.

We do not just assign the jobs and fold our arms. We ensure strict supervision, making sure quality jobs are delivered at the agreed time.

We try to minimize disappointment during the cause of project execution. However, in the event of this, Rafield Limited returns your money immediately if project can not be executed as planned. So you are always protected!
